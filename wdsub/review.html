GPO is a roblox game based on the anime One piece. One piece follows a charcter named Monkey D. Luffy and his Straw Hat Pirates as they search for the ultimate treasure, the "One Piece," to make Luffy the King of the Pirates. In GPO your goal is to obtain fruits,fighting styles, guns,acessories, and swords to fight npcs to level up or other players for bounty.

Top 5 GPO PVE fruits

DRAGON

The Uo Uo no Mi, Model: Seiryu (Fish-Fish Fruit, Model: Azure Dragon) is a Mythical Zoan-type Devil Fruit that gives its user the ability to transform into a hybrid and full version of an Azure Dragon at will. It is called "Dragon-Dragon" in-game and simply referred as "Dragon" in inventory. The Dragon Fruit in GPO is one of the most powerful fruits currently in the game. It uses a rage bar system that fills over time and builds faster during combat. Rage is required to enter Hybrid and Full Dragon forms, and once transformed, your speed, and damage increase dramatically. Even in base (human) form, Dragon is extremely strong.

Moveset:

Base form

  • Hellfire Ring (E) – A quick close-range flame slash that is reliable for combo extensions.
  • Draconic Warcry (R) – A wide-area roar that controls space and breaks enemy pressure.
  • Thunderclad Veil (B) – A damage amplification buff that increases combo damage.
  • Z Move – A heavy flame strike with higher damage and stronger animation.
  • T Move Damage Buff (B) – An enhanced damage amplifier

    • Hybrid form

  • Thunder Strike (E) – A lightning slam that dazes enemies and guard breaks.
  • Draconic Thunderfall (R) – A powerful burst lightning attack.
  • Damage Buff (B) – An enhanced damage amplifier in Hybrid form.
  • Heavy Flame Slam (Z) – A stronger, wider flame slam attack.
  • Mark of Thunder (X) – Marks enemies and strikes them with delayed lightning.
  • Hellfire Ray – A long-range flame beam used for sniping enemies.

    • Full form

  • Hellfire Ray – A long-range flame beam used for sniping enemies.
  • Basic Fire Breath (Tap) – Continuous flame spray for farming and pressure.
  • Hellfire Mode (E) – Enhances abilities with burn effects and increased damage.
  • Roar / Flame Rain (R) – A large AoE scream that rains lightning or fire.
  • Fire Tornado (Z) – Lifts enemies into the air and deals sustained damage.
  • Dragon Slam (X) – A massive AoE slam that sends enemies flying.
  • Ultimate – Hellfire Blast Breath – A chargeable, long-range attack with extreme damage capable of wiping entire areas.

PIKA

The The Pika Pika no Mi (Light-Light Fruit) is a Legendary Logia-type Devil Fruit that grants the user the ability to manipulate, produce, and turn into light at will. The fruit glows yellow and looks like a star. As a Logia-type, it grants the user a "Logia bar", which can be trained to dodge up to 20 non-Haki-infused physical attacks by draining it repeatedly. Additionally, it turns the Q dash into a short, instantaneous light dash. Many argue this dash is worse than the normal one.

Moveset:

  • Light Ray (M1 Tap)
  • Light Ray – A fast light projectile that can be used for ranged pressure and combo extensions.
  • Mirror Kick – A multi-hit light kick attack that is strong for combo damage.
  • Light Barrage – A rapid series of light projectiles that deal sustained damage.
  • Light Beam – A powerful long-range beam used for sniping and high burst damage.
  • Light Flight – Allows fast aerial movement, making travel and repositioning extremely easy.

I like the light barrage as it does so much damage. Its really good for PVE but its not the best for pvp

SOUL

The Soru Soru no Mi (Soul-Soul Fruit) is a Mythical Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that gives its user the ability to interact with and manipulate souls at will, based off Big Mom from One Piece. This fruit features a Soul Bar wherein damage dealt with Soru's abilities fills this bar, this Soul Bar does not have passive regen and requires a minimum of 100 (200 max) souls to activate Soul Rage. Activating Soul Rage drains the Soul Bar rapidly and will not increase when dealing damage with Soru, emptying this bar will disable Soul Rage. Synergizes with the Soul Cleaver by unlocking two more moves, Soul Cleaver abilities add to your soul bar.

Moveset:

  • E – Soul Swap – Swaps your moveset between Zeus and Prometheus. Can be used even while one of them is mid-attack.
  • R – Thunder Drop (Zeus) – Calls down a lightning strike at your mouse’s location for strong burst damage.
  • T – Nimbus Ride (Zeus) – Allows you to fly on Zeus. Prometheus moves can still be used while flying. Using Thunder Drop while airborne strikes directly below you. Certain Zeus abilities and Soul Rage cannot be used while flying.
  • Z – Storm Circuit (Zeus) – Places clouds connected by lightning that stun and explode on contact. Multiple clouds can be placed before they activate.
  • X – Storm Chaser (Zeus) – Sends Zeus to grab a target and slam them down, acting as a strong combo extender.
  • C – Soul Rage – Enters an enraged mode that unlocks enhanced Zeus and Prometheus abilities for increased damage and pressure.
  • Y – Soul Summoning – Swaps between Zeus and Prometheus movesets instantly, even if one is currently attacking.
  • Passive – Soul Bar – Landing moves fills the Soul Bar based on damage ticks dealt. Souls collected are invisible to other players.

The soul fruit isnt my favorite but it s realy good for PVE and PVP. I love the x move which is auto aims and stuns for a long time. It is also great for megladon stacking as it is immune to thunder which strikes frequently.

MAGU

he Magu Magu no Mi (Magma-Magma Fruit)The Magu Magu no Mi (Magma-Magma Fruit) is a Legendary Logia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to create, control, and transform into magma at will. The fruit is known for its strong AoE abilities. The fruit has a unique appearance. It looks like an apple made from molten rock over in hot orange and red magma Magu-Magu is an extremely powerful fruit in PvE due to its high damage output. However, it also excels in PvP since most of its moves deal a high amount of damage and stun the opponent. Many people call the fruit broken due to its high of amounts of damage, AOE on most moves and stun rate. The downsides of the fruit, however are that the moves are slow and as well as little combo potential beyond the R move. The fruit should mainly be used for fruit mains. As a Logia-type, it grants the user a "Logia bar", which can be trained to dodge up to 20 non-Haki-infused physical attacks by draining it repeatedly.

Moveset:

  • Passive – Magu Walk – Allows the user to walk on water without taking damage. Activates automatically when walking on water and uses no stamina.
  • E – Magma Swamp – The user transforms into a slow-moving lava puddle that deals damage over time to enemies who touch it. After a short duration, the user erupts from the ground, block-breaking targets in the area.
  • R – Magma Eruption – Slams the ground to create lava spikes beneath enemies in range. The spikes block break and deal strong damage. The size of the eruption scales with mastery and the move has hyperarmor.
  • Z – Magma Fist – Fires a fist-shaped magma projectile that deals high damage and applies burn. The projectile has gravity, requiring higher aim at long distances. It can also damage structures.
  • X – Magma Hound – Launches a faster magma projectile shaped like a hound. It has less gravity than Magma Fist, travels quicker, has a larger AoE, and can damage structures.
  • C – Magma Rain – Charges and unleashes a massive barrage of lava projectiles over a wide area, dealing devastating damage. This move can also damage structures.

Magu is kind of hard to use but once you can get the aim and timing correct it becomes a very lethal fruit. It is really good for battle royal PVE. I personally prefer pika and dragon over this fruit but it is very lethal. It is easily blockable which takes away some of its points.

VENOM

The Doku Doku No Mi (Poison Poison Fruit) The Doku Doku No Mi (Poison Poison Fruit) is a Mythical Paramecia type Devil Fruit that allows the user to produce and control different variations of poison. Said poison is completely controllable by the user, and may be formed into any shape or size.

Moveset:

  • Passive – Rage Bar – A bar that fills when damaging opponents with Doku moves. At 80% full, it allows the use of Venom Demon.
  • Passive – Poison Puddles – Abilities leave behind toxic pools that deal damage over time and inflict poison on enemies who step into them.
  • E – Venom Spit – Fires a concentrated venom projectile that poisons enemies. Holding the move briefly causes it to block break.
  • R (Tap) – Venom Hydra – Summons a blockable venom hydra that chases enemies and applies pressure.
  • R (Hold) – Venom Hydra – Fires three homing venom projectiles instead of one, tracking nearby targets.
  • T – Venom Road – Allows you to travel through your venom hydra, giving strong mobility and chase potential.
  • Z – Venom Discharge – Releases a powerful block-breaking venom blast around you, damaging nearby enemies.
  • X – Venom Aura – Creates a lingering, blockable poison cloud around you that damages enemies over time.
  • C – Venom Demon – Transforms you into a massive venomous demon avatar, greatly increasing your combat strength and pressure.
