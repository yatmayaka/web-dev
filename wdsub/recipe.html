Chickfila Chicken Strips Recipe (with bbq sauce)

  1. Ingredients

    • 1 -1.5 pounds chicken tenders
    • 1 cup dill pickle juice the liquid
    • 1 large egg
    • 1/4 cup whole milk
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1 tablespoon powdered sugar (aka confectioners)
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon celery powder
    • oil for cooking

  1. Marination

    1. Place the chicken tenders in a freezer zip-top bag
    2. add pickle juice
    3. refrigerate at least 4 hours.
    4. Remove the pickled chicken from the ziplock bag and pat chicken dry with a paper towel.
    5. Add egg and milk to a small bowl and beat.
    6. Add flour, sugar, paprika, salt, pepper, basil, garlic powder, onion powder and celery powder to another bowl.

  1. Cooking steps

    1. Add oil to a large skillet or saute pan to 1/4 in deep. Heat to medium.
    2. Dredge chicken in egg mixture then in flour mixture.
    3. Add chicken to hot oil and cook 4 to 5 minutes per side until golden brown.
    4. Drain on paper towels
